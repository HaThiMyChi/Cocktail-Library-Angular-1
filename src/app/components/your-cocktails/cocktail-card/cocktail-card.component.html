Mezcal Fun

by Lisa

60 ml Mezcal
90 ml blood orange soda
60 ml sparkling water
30 ml lemon juice
a couple of thyme sprigs
worm salt
blood orange slices

Cut a lime wedge to wet the rim of your cocktail glass. Then, dip the rim into a bowl of worm salt. In a shaker, pour in mezcal and lime juice. Shake for 10 seconds. Pour into a salt-rimmed and ice-filled cocktail glass.Top off with blood orange soda and sparkling water. Garnish with a slice of blood orange and a couple sprigs of thyme.

Gingerbread House

by Ioan

60 ml spiced rum
10 ml triple sec
5 ml Masala Chai syrup
10 ml lemon juice

Mix all the ingredients in a shaker with a couple of ice cubes. Shake 15 seconds. Strain and pour over ice. Fill the rest of the glass with coke.
